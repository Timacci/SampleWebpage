Meowth, that's right!

Meowth are not your average felines. Having a Meowth by your side, you'll find yourself in good company. They can learn various Normal-type moves, and once you evolve yours into a Persian, you'll have a wide range of moves from various types to choose from through the use of some TMs.

  • They have a big golden coin on their foreheads!
  • Their exclusive "Pay Day" move will make you rich in no time!
  • A few Fury Swipes can really intimidate smaller foes!

    • Gotta Have Sweets!

    Did you know Meowth loves to eat sweets? Here are some typical Meowth favorites!

    Old Gateau

    A small cake with a spooky origin. It'll scare your hunger away!

    Rage Candy Bar

    A sweet piece of candy that's all the rage right now.

    Sitrus Berry

    This tasty fruit heals a lot of HP for the tired Meowth.

    Castelia Cone

    A scoop of this is sure to bring you dreams of the big city.

    Watch out! It's Weavile!

    Weavile likes to act like a tough guy, but they are sure to be a great partner Pokémon! Devious and intelligent, Weavile communicate with each other by carving patterns on trees and rocks to take down their prey in packs of four or five. Just hope you're not on the receiving end of their attack!

  • Beware of its sharp and curved claws!
  • Their blazing speed leaves their foes exhausted!
